# GMAT Vocab Wizard

Mobile-first vocab lookup, learning, and testing for GMAT prep.

**Live site:** [https://gmat-prep-36738.web.app](https://gmat-prep-36738.web.app)

---

## How to use the website

1. **Sign in**  
   Use **Sign in with Google** (top right). You need to be signed in to save words and use Learn/Test.

2. **Lookup (home)**  
   - Enter a **word or phrase** (e.g. *obdurate* or *on the verge of*) and tap **Generate**.  
   - The app generates a definition, simple definition, example, synonyms, and GMAT usage notes.  
   - Tap **Save** to add it to your library.

3. **Learn**  
   - Open **Learn** from the bottom nav.  
   - Use filters: **All**, **Do Not Know**, **Learning**, **Know**, **Flagged**.  
   - Search by text.  
   - On each card you can change status, flag, delete, or expand details.  
   - **Flashcards:** switch to Flashcards to flip through the current list one card at a time (Show/Hide answer, Prev/Next).  
   - **Paragraph:** switch to Paragraph, then **Generate paragraph**. The app picks 5 random words from “Do Not Know” + “Learning”, builds a short paragraph, and **bolds** those words. **Hover** a bold word to see its meaning in a tooltip.

4. **Test**  
   - Open **Test**, choose **Meaning** or **GMAT-style**, pick 5 or 10 questions, and run the quiz.  
   - After finishing you get a score and can review correct answers.
